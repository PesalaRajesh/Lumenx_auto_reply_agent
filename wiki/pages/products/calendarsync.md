# CalendarSync

**Booking links that respect your day.**

CalendarSync empowers your team with polished, intelligent booking links. Each teammate gets a personal booking page with built-in protections—buffer time between meetings, daily meeting caps, and automatic conflict detection. Attendees always see real, available time slots.

## Overview

CalendarSync is designed for sales, recruiting, and any role that manages frequent meetings. It eliminates back-and-forth scheduling by letting guests pick from genuinely free calendar slots in real time.

### Key Features

- **Personal booking pages** — Each team member gets their own branded booking link
- **Round-robin and collective scheduling** — Distribute meetings fairly across your team or book with groups
- **Daily meeting caps and buffer time** — Set maximum meetings per day and automatic breaks between appointments
- **Video conference integration** — Automatically generate Zoom, Google Meet, and Microsoft Teams links
- **Two-way calendar sync** — Real-time synchronisation with Google Calendar and Microsoft 365

## Common Customer Questions

**Q: Can multiple team members use the same CalendarSync account?**  
A: Yes. Team and Pro plans support multiple users. Use the Team plan for larger groups with per-seat pricing.

**Q: What happens if I reach my daily meeting cap?**  
A: Your booking page will automatically hide additional time slots once the cap is reached, preventing overbooking.

**Q: Does CalendarSync work with multiple calendars?**  
A: Yes. You can sync both personal and shared calendars from Google Calendar and Microsoft 365.

**Q: Can I customise the buffer time between meetings?**  
A: Yes. CalendarSync lets you set custom buffer durations (e.g., 15 minutes) between back-to-back bookings.

**Q: What if I need to cancel an upcoming booking?**  
A: Cancellations are managed through your CalendarSync dashboard. Attendees are automatically notified of changes.
