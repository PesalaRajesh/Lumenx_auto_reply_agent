# ReceiptVault

**Snap, store, and reclaim every receipt.**

ReceiptVault transforms paper receipts and email confirmations into searchable, organized expense records. Using advanced OCR technology, it automatically extracts merchant names, amounts, currencies, and tax information—turning chaos into clarity in seconds.

## Overview

ReceiptVault is designed for founders, traveling professionals, and finance operations teams who need to track expenses without the administrative burden. Whether you're capturing receipts on the go or forwarding email confirmations, ReceiptVault centralizes everything in one searchable vault.

## Core Features

- **Mobile Receipt Capture** — Take photos of receipts directly from your phone
- **Email Forwarding Ingestion** — Forward receipt emails to your ReceiptVault inbox for automatic processing
- **OCR Extraction** — Automatic detection of merchant, amount, currency, and tax details
- **Per-Project Tagging** — Organize receipts by project, client, or category for easy retrieval
- **Accounting Tool Sync** — Direct integration with QuickBooks, Zoho Books, and Tally

## Common Customer Questions

**Q: Can I capture receipts offline?**  
A: The mobile app allows offline capture. Receipt processing occurs once you reconnect to the internet.

**Q: What currencies does ReceiptVault support?**  
A: OCR extraction handles multiple currencies automatically. Check your integrations for currency conversion support.

**Q: How do I switch plans?**  
A: You can upgrade or downgrade at any time. Billing adjusts pro-rata.

**Q: Is there a limit to how long I can keep receipts in my vault?**  
A: No, storage is unlimited. You only pay for monthly receipt processing.

**Q: Can multiple team members access the same vault?**  
A: Yes, the Team plan includes up to 10 seats for shared access.
