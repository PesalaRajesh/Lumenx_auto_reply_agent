# BillSplit

## Overview

BillSplit delivers subscription billing, dunning, and invoicing infrastructure—typically a quarter-long engineering project—ready to deploy in a weekend. Purpose-built for indie SaaS founders and small SaaS companies, it includes hosted checkout, customer portal, and webhook events out of the box.

## Key Features

- **Hosted Checkout & Portal** – White-labeled payment collection and customer self-service management
- **Dunning & Retry Policy** – Automated payment failure recovery to reduce churn
- **Per-Country Tax Handling** – Toggle tax compliance rules by region with a single switch
- **Multi-Backend Support** – Stripe and Razorpay integrations for global payment processing
- **Webhook Event Stream** – Real-time billing events for downstream system synchronization

## Common Customer Questions

**Q: Can I use BillSplit with my existing payment processor?**  
A: Yes. BillSplit integrates with both Stripe and Razorpay. If you already use one of these, you can connect it directly.

**Q: What happens to my customer data if I cancel?**  
A: Your complete dataset—customers, invoices, and billing history—exports as a single ZIP file that you can download and store independently.

**Q: Do I need to handle tax compliance myself?**  
A: No. BillSplit includes per-country tax handling. Enable it per region with a single toggle, and tax rules apply automatically.

**Q: Which plan should I choose?**  
A: Start with Starter if your monthly revenue is under the cap. Upgrade to Pro or Team as your recurring revenue grows. See current pricing for exact revenue thresholds.

**Q: How quickly will billing issues be resolved?**  
A: LumenX aims to respond to all support requests within 12 hours.
