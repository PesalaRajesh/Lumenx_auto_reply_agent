# TimeMark

## Overview

TimeMark is a quiet time tracker designed for freelancers and small agencies. Unlike intrusive time-tracking tools, TimeMark works in the background—automatically detecting what you're working on and organizing your time by project at the end of each day. It integrates seamlessly with [[InvoiceFlow]] to turn tracked time into invoices with just one click.

**Category:** Productivity  
**Best for:** Freelancers and small teams

## Key Features

- **Auto-detection** – Intelligently identifies which project you're actively working on
- **Manual entries** – Add time manually when needed
- **Per-project rates** – Set different hourly rates for each project
- **Weekly digests** – Get a summary of your week's work delivered to your inbox
- **Invoice integration** – One-click handoff to [[InvoiceFlow]] for instant invoicing

## Common Customer Questions

**Q: Can I switch between plans?**  
A: Yes. You can upgrade or downgrade at any time. Changes take effect on your next billing cycle.

**Q: Does TimeMark work offline?**  
A: TimeMark requires an internet connection to sync project data, but the auto-detection engine runs locally on your device.

**Q: How do I import time from another tracker?**  
A: Use the Toggl import feature, or export your data as CSV and contact support for manual migration assistance.

**Q: What happens to my data if I cancel?**  
A: Your data remains accessible for export during the refund window. After that, archived accounts are retained for 90 days before permanent deletion.

**Q: Can I set different rates for different clients?**  
A: Yes. TimeMark supports per-project hourly rates, so you can bill different clients at different rates and track them all in one place.
