# LinkVault

## Overview

LinkVault is a shared bookmark manager designed for teams that need to maintain organized, accessible collections of links without the hassle of dead URLs. Saved links are automatically tagged and verified weekly, ensuring your team always has access to current, working resources.

**Best for:** Research, design, and engineering teams who collaborate on projects requiring frequent link sharing and management.

## Key Features

- **Browser Extension & Slack Integration** — Save and search links directly from Chrome, Firefox, or Safari, or use Slack slash commands for seamless workflow integration
- **Auto-Tagging** — Links are automatically tagged based on page content, reducing manual organization work
- **Weekly Dead-Link Detection** — Broken URLs are identified and flagged automatically every week
- **Per-Collection Sharing** — Share specific link collections with team members or keep them private
- **Read Later Queue** — Build a personal queue for links you want to review later

## Common Customer Questions

**Q: Can I use LinkVault without the browser extension?**  
A: Yes. You can access and search your bookmarks via the Slack slash command, though the browser extension offers the fastest save experience.

**Q: What happens when a dead link is detected?**  
A: LinkVault flags the link in your collection. You'll receive a summary during the weekly sweep so you can remove or update it.

**Q: How many team members can I invite on the Starter plan?**  
A: The Starter plan supports up to 3 seats.

**Q: Can I export my bookmarks?**  
A: Please contact support for export options and available formats.

**Q: What's the difference between Pro and Team plans?**  
A: Pro supports up to 10 team members with a fixed monthly rate. Team plans offer per-seat pricing for organizations needing more than 10 members.
