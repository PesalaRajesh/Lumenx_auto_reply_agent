# SignPath

## Overview

SignPath is a legally valid e-signature solution designed for small teams that need to collect signatures without paying per-document fees. With a flat monthly subscription, you can drag signature fields onto PDFs, manage multiple signers, and maintain complete audit trails.

**Best for:** Sales ops, HR, and legal teams in small companies.

## Key Features

- **Drag-to-place signature fields** – Intuitive interface for positioning signature boxes on any PDF
- **Sequential and parallel signers** – Route documents to one signer at a time or request multiple signatures simultaneously
- **Audit trail with IP and timestamp** – Track every interaction with legally defensible documentation
- **Bulk send capabilities** – Send to up to 200 recipients in a single batch
- **Pre-built templates** – Common contract templates to accelerate workflows
- **Default audit logging** – No need to enable separately; included in all plans

## Common Customer Questions

**Q: Can I use SignPath for one-off document signing, or do I need a monthly commitment?**
A: SignPath operates on a monthly subscription model. However, you can cancel anytime without penalties, and all signed documents remain permanently accessible.

**Q: What happens if I exceed my monthly document limit?**
A: Exceeding your plan's document limit depends on your specific tier. Consider upgrading to Pro or Team if you regularly hit limits.

**Q: Are the signatures legally binding?**
A: Yes. SignPath collects legally valid e-signatures with full audit trail support, making documents defensible for legal purposes.

**Q: Can multiple team members access the same account?**
A: Only the Team plan includes multiple seats (10 users). Starter and Pro plans are single-user.

**Q: How do I migrate signatures from another e-signature platform?**
A: Contact support for assistance with migration. All your existing signed documents can be archived in your preferred cloud storage.
