# InboxClean

## Overview

InboxClean is an email management tool designed to help busy professionals triage their inbox in just three minutes a day. The system automatically organizes emails into three categories—**Now**, **Later**, and **Forever-Maybe**—making it easy to prioritize what matters most.

**Best for:** Executives, founders, and consultants managing high-volume inboxes.

## Key Features

- **Now, Later, Maybe triage view** — Automatically categorizes incoming emails so you can focus on urgent messages first
- **One-tap bulk unsubscribe** — Instantly remove yourself from multiple unwanted mailing lists
- **Sender reputation scoring** — Identifies trustworthy senders and flags potential spam or phishing
- **Promotions auto-filing** — Automatically routes marketing emails away from your primary inbox
- **Snooze with smart resurface** — Temporarily hide emails and have them reappear when you need them

## Common Customer Questions

**Q: Will InboxClean delete my emails?**  
No. InboxClean only organizes and filters your inbox—it never deletes your messages. All your emails remain safely stored in your email provider.

**Q: Which email provider should I use?**  
InboxClean supports Gmail and Outlook. Choose whichever you currently use; no migration needed.

**Q: Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?**  
Yes. You can change plans anytime. Your billing will adjust accordingly on your next billing cycle.

**Q: What happens to my data after I cancel?**  
Your emails remain untouched on your email provider's servers. InboxClean's organizational rules and settings are removed, but your actual messages are never affected.
