# PollWise

## Overview

PollWise is a streamlined survey tool designed for product, marketing, and research teams who need to gather quick feedback without complexity. Create and distribute polls in under a minute, then access instant results with powerful analysis features.

**Key tagline:** Run a poll in under a minute.

## Core Features

- **Single or multi-question polls** — Build simple feedback requests or more detailed surveys
- **Multiple distribution methods** — Share via direct link, email, or QR code
- **Live result dashboards** — Watch responses come in real-time
- **Cross-tabs and filters** — Slice and dice results by respondent segments
- **Anonymous mode** — Collect honest feedback without identifying respondents

## Common Customer Questions

**Q: Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan anytime?**  
A: Yes. Changes take effect at your next billing cycle.

**Q: What's the difference between Pro and Team plans?**  
A: Team includes unlimited responses per poll and 10 team seats, ideal for organizations running multiple concurrent polls. Pro is best for individuals or small teams with moderate volume.

**Q: Can I export my poll data?**  
A: Yes. CSV exports are available on all paid plans, making it easy to analyze data in spreadsheets or other tools.

**Q: Does PollWise work on mobile?**  
A: PollWise is fully responsive. Respondents can complete polls on any device, and QR code distribution is especially useful for mobile audiences.

**Q: What happens to my data if I cancel?**  
A: Your poll data remains accessible during the (see current policy in product JSON) refund window. After that, archived data retention depends on your plan—contact support for details.
