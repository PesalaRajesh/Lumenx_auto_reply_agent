# ChatRelay

## Overview

ChatRelay is a lightweight customer chat solution designed for small support teams. It combines an embeddable website chat widget with a unified inbox, eliminating the need for complex communication infrastructure. Connect email, WhatsApp, and Slack to manage all customer conversations in one place.

**Best for:** Small support teams, e-commerce shops, and SaaS startups.

## Key Features

- **Embeddable chat widget** — Add a professional chat interface to your website in minutes
- **Multi-channel inbox** — Centralize messages from email, WhatsApp, and other connected platforms
- **Topic routing** — Automatically direct conversations to the right team member
- **Business-hours auto-reply** — Set automatic responses when your team is offline
- **Saved replies and shortcuts** — Speed up responses with pre-written messages and keyboard shortcuts

## Common Customer Questions

**Q: Can I export my chat history if I cancel?**  
A: Yes. Your entire inbox history exports as JSON, giving you full data portability.

**Q: Do I need to commit to a contract?**  
A: No. All plans are month-to-month and can be cancelled anytime.

**Q: What happens to chats when business hours end?**  
A: Customers receive an automated reply letting them know your team is offline. Messages are queued in your inbox for when you're back online.

**Q: Can I add more team members if I outgrow my plan?**  
A: Yes. Upgrade to Pro (5 seats) or Team (20 seats), or contact support for custom arrangements.

**Q: Does ChatRelay work on mobile?**  
A: The embedded widget works on all devices. Team members can manage conversations via the web inbox or connect through Slack for mobile access.
