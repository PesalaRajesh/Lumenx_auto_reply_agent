# TeamPulse

## Overview

TeamPulse is a lightweight pulse survey tool designed for teams to gather continuous feedback at regular intervals. Unlike traditional annual engagement surveys, TeamPulse delivers actionable insights through short, recurring surveys that take approximately 90 seconds to complete.

**Best for:** People ops and engineering managers seeking real-time team sentiment and engagement trends.

## Key Features

- **Flexible survey cadence** — Run surveys weekly, biweekly, or monthly
- **Anonymous by default** — Encourage honest feedback with built-in anonymity
- **Five-person aggregation floor** — Data is aggregated and only displayed when responses represent at least 5 people, protecting individual privacy
- **eNPS and custom themes** — Measure employee Net Promoter Score or design custom survey questions
- **Slack and Teams delivery** — Push surveys directly to teams where they work

## How It Works

TeamPulse automates recurring feedback collection through your preferred communication platform. Survey responses are aggregated automatically, and trends emerge over weeks rather than requiring a full annual engagement cycle. The five-person minimum ensures anonymity while providing meaningful team-level insights.

## Common Customer Questions

**Q: Can responses be traced back to individuals?**
A: No. TeamPulse is anonymous by default, and data is only displayed when aggregated above the five-person floor to maintain confidentiality.

**Q: Which communication platform should we use?**
A: TeamPulse supports both Slack and Microsoft Teams. Choose whichever platform your team uses most frequently.

**Q: How long does it take employees to complete a survey?**
A: Approximately 90 seconds per survey.

**Q: Can we export our survey data?**
A: Yes. Upon cancellation or anytime, your full aggregated history exports as CSV.

**Q: What's included in the Starter plan?**
A: The free Starter plan supports up to 10 seats with full TeamPulse functionality.
