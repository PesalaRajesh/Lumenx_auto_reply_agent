# EmailPilot

## Overview

EmailPilot is an AI-powered email assistant that drafts replies matching your unique tone and style. By analyzing your past sent messages and reading attached file context, EmailPilot generates intelligent draft responses—keeping you in full control since every reply remains a draft until you explicitly send it.

**Best for:** Founders, consultants, and customer-facing professionals managing high-volume inboxes.

## Key Features

- **Tone-matched draft replies** — AI learns your writing style and voice
- **Attachment-aware context** — Reads documents and files to inform drafts
- **Per-thread summary** — Quick overview of email conversation threads
- **Snooze and follow-up reminders** — Never miss important messages
- **Multi-platform support** — Works seamlessly with Gmail and Outlook

## Common Customer Questions

**Q: Does EmailPilot send emails automatically?**  
A: No. EmailPilot only drafts replies. You review every message before sending—you maintain full control.

**Q: Can I use EmailPilot with both Gmail and Outlook?**  
A: Yes, EmailPilot supports both Gmail and Outlook. See [[email_provider_setup]] for platform-specific configuration.

**Q: What happens to my drafts if I cancel?**  
A: Your account access remains active until the end of your billing period. You can download or export drafts during this window.

**Q: Does EmailPilot read my attachments?**  
A: Yes. EmailPilot uses attachment content to provide context-aware draft suggestions. All processing respects your privacy—see [[privacy_policy]] for details.

**Q: Is there a draft limit on the Team plan?**  
A: No, the Team plan offers unlimited drafts per month. Starter and Pro plans have monthly caps.
