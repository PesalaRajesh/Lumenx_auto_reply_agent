# PixelDeck

Slides built in markdown, exported as pixels.

## Overview

PixelDeck is a presentation tool designed for engineers, educators, and technical founders who want polished decks without the friction of traditional slide editors. Write your content in markdown, and PixelDeck handles the design—no dragging text boxes required.

Export your work as PDF, PPTX, or a shareable hosted URL.

## Key Features

- **Markdown editor with live preview** – Write naturally, see results instantly
- **20 themes with 5 color schemes** – Professional designs out of the box
- **Multiple export formats** – PDF, PPTX, or hosted URL
- **Speaker notes** – Keep your talking points alongside slides
- **Diagram blocks** – Embed flowcharts, sequence diagrams, and Mermaid diagrams directly in markdown
