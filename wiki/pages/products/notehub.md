# NoteHub

## Overview

NoteHub is a markdown-first notes application designed for researchers, writers, and engineering teams who need their notes to be discoverable and interconnected. With backlinks, graph visualization, and local-first search, NoteHub helps you build a personal knowledge base that grows more valuable over time.

**Tagline:** Notes you can actually find later.

## Key Features

- **Markdown Editor with Live Preview** — Write in markdown and see formatted output instantly
- **Backlinks and Graph View** — Automatically cross-reference pages and visualize connections between notes
- **Local-First Search** — Lightning-fast search that runs on your device, even with 10,000+ notes
- **Daily Journal Template** — Built-in structure for journaling and daily notes
- **End-to-End Encrypted Sync** — Your notes stay private while syncing across devices

## Common Customer Questions

**Q: Can I use NoteHub offline?**
A: Yes! NoteHub is local-first, meaning your notes sync to your device and remain accessible even without an internet connection.

**Q: What happens to my notes if I cancel?**
A: Your notes stay with you. Even after cancellation, you can export them or access them offline forever.

**Q: Can I collaborate with my team on notes?**
A: Yes, the Team plan includes unlimited shared spaces designed for team collaboration.

**Q: How much data can I store?**
A: The Starter plan supports up to 200 notes. The Pro plan offers unlimited notes storage.

**Q: Is my data encrypted?**
A: Yes, NoteHub uses end-to-end encryption for all synced data, ensuring your notes stay private.
