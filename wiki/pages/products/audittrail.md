# AuditTrail

## Overview

AuditTrail is a compliance logging solution designed for engineering and security teams in regulated industries. It records who did what, where, and when across your stack, generating SOC 2 and ISO 27001 evidence packs on demand without requiring external consulting.

**Key promise:** Drop in one SDK and transform your logs into audit-ready records.

## Core Features

- **Append-only event log** — Immutable record of all activity
- **SOC 2 and ISO 27001 evidence packs** — Generated on demand for compliance audits
- **Per-user activity timeline** — Track individual user actions across your system
- **Configurable retention** — Adjust log retention to meet your compliance requirements
- **Multi-language SDKs** — Native support for Node, Python, and Go

## Integrations

AuditTrail connects with:
- AWS CloudTrail
- Datadog
- Splunk
- Webhooks (custom integrations)
