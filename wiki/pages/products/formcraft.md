# FormCraft

## Overview

FormCraft helps you build conversational forms that double completion rates. Instead of traditional form layouts, FormCraft uses smart logic jumps, hidden fields, and partial response capture to create engaging form experiences. All submissions flow into a clean dashboard or route to any webhook you specify.

**Best for:** Marketers, researchers, and customer success teams collecting customer data, feedback, and insights.

## Key Features

- **Conversational and classic layouts** – Choose the form style that matches your brand and use case
- **Logic jumps and hidden fields** – Personalise form flows based on user responses
- **Partial response capture** – Save incomplete submissions so no data is lost
- **File uploads up to 50 MB** – Collect documents, images, and media directly in your forms
- **Webhook and Zapier output** – Integrate form responses with your existing tools and workflows

## Common Customer Questions

**Q: Can I upgrade or downgrade plans mid-month?**
A: Yes. You can change your plan anytime, and billing adjusts automatically.

**Q: What happens if I exceed my monthly response limit?**
A: Refer to your plan details in the billing section. You can upgrade to a higher tier anytime.

**Q: Are my form responses secure?**
A: All submissions are encrypted in transit and at rest. Webhooks and integrations use secure authentication. See [[Data Security]] for more information.

**Q: Can I use FormCraft for conditional logic across many fields?**
A: Yes. Logic jumps let you hide, show, or skip fields based on previous answers, supporting complex form workflows.

**Q: What file types can I upload?**
A: You can upload files up to 50 MB. Most common formats are supported (PDF, images, documents). Check the dashboard for the full list.
