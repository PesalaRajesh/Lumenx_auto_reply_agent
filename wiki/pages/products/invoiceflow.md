# InvoiceFlow

## Overview

InvoiceFlow transforms invoice creation from a tedious task into a streamlined, automated process. Generate polished, branded invoices from simple descriptions in seconds, and watch payments arrive faster with built-in reminders and multiple payout options.

**Best for:** Freelancers, agencies, and small service businesses.

## Key Features

- **Branded invoice templates** – Customise invoices to match your brand identity
- **Multi-currency support** – Work with clients worldwide
- **Automatic late reminders** – Reminders sent on day 3, 7, and 14 after due date
- **Direct payouts** – Payments flow straight to your bank via Stripe or Razorpay
- **Compliance ready** – GST and VAT compliant invoicing

## Common Customer Questions

**Q: Can I switch plans mid-month?**  
A: Yes. Plan changes take effect immediately, and billing adjusts pro-rata.

**Q: What happens to my invoices if I cancel?**  
A: Your invoices remain accessible for export as CSV for 30 days after cancellation. After that period, contact support if you need historical data.

**Q: Do you support currencies other than USD?**  
A: Yes. InvoiceFlow supports multi-currency invoicing, allowing you to invoice clients in their local currency.

**Q: Which payment processor should I choose—Stripe or Razorpay?**  
A: Both are fully integrated. Choose based on your geography and which processor you already use; you can also set up both.

A: Check the [[pricing]] page or contact sales for current trial availability.
