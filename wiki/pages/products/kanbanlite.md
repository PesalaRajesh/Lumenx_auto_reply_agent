# KanbanLite

## Overview

KanbanLite is a streamlined kanban board tool designed for teams that want visual task management without unnecessary complexity. With a focus on essential features and keyboard efficiency, KanbanLite helps engineering and design teams maintain clear workflow visibility.

**Tagline:** A kanban board, nothing more.

## Key Features

- **Unlimited boards** on paid plans
- **Keyboard-driven card creation** for fast workflow
- **Single label per card** — intentional design simplicity
- **Public read-only links** for sharing board views
- **JSON import and export** for data portability

## Common Customer Questions

**Q: Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?**  
A: Yes. Plan changes take effect on your next billing cycle. Reach out to support if you need immediate assistance.

**Q: Is there a free trial?**  
A: The Starter plan is permanently free and includes 2 boards, giving you a risk-free way to experience KanbanLite.

**Q: Can I export my data?**  
A: Yes. KanbanLite supports JSON export, allowing you to back up or migrate your boards at any time.

**Q: What happens if I cancel mid-cycle?**  
A: You retain access until the end of your billing period. See our cancellation policy for refund eligibility.

**Q: How many team members can I add?**  
A: Team plan pricing is per seat with a minimum of 5 seats. Starter and Pro plans have no built-in seat limits for board sharing.
