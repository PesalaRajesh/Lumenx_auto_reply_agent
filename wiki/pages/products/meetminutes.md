# MeetMinutes

**Category:** Meetings  
**Tagline:** Meeting notes that actually capture decisions.

## Overview

MeetMinutes automatically joins your Zoom, Meet, or Teams calls and generates structured meeting notes. The tool extracts decisions, action items, and open questions—delivering them to your inbox within five minutes of call end. A searchable transcript archive ensures nothing gets lost.

Ideal for product, sales, and leadership teams managing high-volume meetings.

## Key Features

- **Auto-join via calendar** — Automatically connects to scheduled meetings without manual setup
- **Decision and action-item extraction** — AI identifies and surfaces critical outcomes
- **Speaker-aware transcripts** — Full transcript with speaker attribution for easy reference
- **Searchable archive** — Find past discussions and decisions instantly
- **Privacy mode for muted segments** — Sensitive muted audio is excluded from notes

## Common Customer Questions

**Q: Does MeetMinutes work with my calendar system?**  
A: Yes. MeetMinutes auto-joins meetings directly from your calendar (Google Calendar, Outlook, etc.). No manual invites needed.

**Q: What happens to my transcripts if I cancel?**  
A: Transcripts remain downloadable for 90 days after cancellation. After that period, they are deleted.

**Q: Can I upgrade or downgrade mid-month?**  
A: Yes. Plan changes take effect immediately, and billing adjusts on a pro-rata basis.

**Q: Does MeetMinutes record audio?**  
A: MeetMinutes transcribes your meeting but does not retain audio files by default. Privacy mode excludes muted segments from transcription.

**Q: How accurate are the decision and action-item summaries?**  
A: Summaries are AI-generated and typically capture 90%+ of explicit decisions and assignments. We recommend reviewing the full transcript for complex or ambiguous items.
